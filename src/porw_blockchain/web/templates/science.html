Scientific Impact

The PoRW blockchain uses protein folding as its Proof of Real Work mechanism, contributing to scientific research while securing the blockchain network.

Overview

Protein folding is one of the most important unsolved problems in biology. Proteins are essential molecules that perform a vast array of functions in living organisms. Their function is determined by their three-dimensional structure, which is in turn determined by their amino acid sequence.

Understanding how proteins fold is crucial for:

  • Developing new drugs and treatments for diseases
  • Engineering proteins with new functions
  • Understanding the fundamental processes of life

By mining on the PoRW blockchain, you contribute computational resources to solving protein folding problems, which helps advance scientific research while earning cryptocurrency rewards.

How It Works
  1. Protein Selection

    The network selects proteins for folding based on scientific relevance and computational requirements.

  2. Distributed Computation

    Miners across the network perform protein folding simulations using their computational resources.

  3. Validation

    Results are validated by the network based on energy scores and conformational stability.

  4. Blockchain Integration

    Validated results are incorporated into the blockchain as Proof of Real Work.

  5. Scientific Contribution

    The results are made available to the scientific community for further research.
